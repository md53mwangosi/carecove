About CareCove

Our Story

CareCove is dedicated to bringing you the finest quality sea moss products from the pristine waters of Zanzibar, Tanzania. Our mission is to promote health and wellness through natural, sustainably sourced sea moss.

Why Sea Moss?

Sea moss is a nutrient-dense superfood packed with 92 of the 102 minerals our bodies need. It's known for supporting immune function, digestive health, and skin vitality.
